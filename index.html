You May Wonder

Is BogenTrack available for both iOS and Android?

Its even better! Since BogenTrack is a web application, you can use it for both your computer or tablet, or any iOS and Android devices as long as you can open a link. Your data is not connected to specific device providing extra flexibility.

Are there any limitations on the free plan?

Great news here - due to the beta launch, BogenTrack is completely free now with all functionality available. In future, specific plans will come with only essential or advanced features. Existing accounts will have extra discount.

Is my data safe and secure on BogenTrack?

Absolutely! We prioritize data security. All your information is stored in the cloud with encryption, ensuring it remains private and accessible only to you. We do not store any sensitive information ourselves.

Can I use BogenTrack offline?

While BogenTrack requires an internet connection to get and send data, unfortunately you can not access recorded sessions and rounds in offline mode for now. We're aware you might be shooting in the wild and working on offline mode development.

Can I export my data from BogenTrack?

Yes, you can export all your archery training data in .json format for backup or analysis purposes. Additional formats are about to be accessible as well.

Can I switch between plans at any time?

BogenTrack is completely free now. When paid functionality update introduced, you will be able to upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime based on your archery needs and preferences.

Can I provide feedback or suggest new features for BogenTrack?

Of course! We value your input. Feel free to send us your feedback and feature suggestions through the app's feedback / reporting functionality in Profile screen, or say "Hello" via email!
